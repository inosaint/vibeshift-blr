## Image Specs

- **Format:** PNG with transparent background
- **Size:** ~100x100px (will display as small avatar next to the speech bubble)
- **Style:** Keep it consistent — rounded, cartoonish, pastel palette matching the unicorn theme
- **Naming convention:** `personalities/01-mysterious-oracle.png`, `personalities/02-bored-librarian.png`, etc.
